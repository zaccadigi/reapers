In the heart of the Grim Underworld, resided a community of Reapers living amongst eachother. They were all competing to eventually earn the title of Grim Reaper and rule the Underworld. There are many types of Reapers, from jesters and mimes for the current Grim Reaper's pleasure, to Astronaut Reapers collecting the souls in another universe. But they all shared the common goal to eventually collect enough souls to solidify the Grim Reaper status. Although competitiveness is always high, the love between each Reaper never breaks. Staying solid as a unit is what keeps them powerful and in control of the souls.

The community consisted of every kind of personality you could imagine! From Athletes, and track runners, to Reapers in mech suits with futuristic scythes. There was everything, and each one had its own role in the community to keep it functioning. The Reapers main objective was to collect as many souls from as many different places as possible. Climbing the leaderboards of who has the most souls captured to eventually takeover the Grim Reaper's throne. The Grim Reaper watches and instructs everything in the Underworld. He is the true leader of all the Reapers and makes the final decision as to where the captured souls will go, Heaven or Hell...

The other communities include the Angel's from the Sanctuary above the clouds, the Devil's from The Pits of Hell, and some stranded souls from the hauntings still on Earth! The communities don't normally join in activities together but there is a small group made up of an Angel, Devil, Boogieman, Ghost, and Reaper. This group of friends has a plan to reconnect each of the communities back together like it once was. They also want to help Boogieman and Ghost finally get off earth and return to their rightful places after being stuck haunting earth for centuries.

We will be releasing our white paper as we go along as to not over promise and under achieve.
